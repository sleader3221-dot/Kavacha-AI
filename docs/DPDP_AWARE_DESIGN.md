# DPDP-Aware Design

Kavacha AI is a DPDP-aware design prototype. It does not claim legal compliance certification.

Controls:

- data minimisation
- purpose limitation
- RBAC
- audit trail
- sensitive case masking
- retention policy placeholder
- officer accountability
- human approval workflow

System banner:

```text
Kavacha AI does not predict individual criminality. It supports area/time/category-level planning only.
```
